Client Build

Consultation

The first step of this project was consulting my client. By working out three different types of builds, catagorised by price and use case, I could deliver a variety of options for my client to choose from. The three different builds were made up of a budget, upper middle class and high end variant which are listed below.

Specs

After my client had established that the mid-class variant was the most suitable for his needs, the parts were ordered and delivered. The specifications of this build would enable the final computer to handle big workloads as well as run most AAA games with ease, checking every requirement.
