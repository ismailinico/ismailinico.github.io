Work process

How I work

Who doesn't like coffee

In our first meeting, be it digital or personal, we will discuss your general ideas and guidelines for the project.

Notes and notes and notes

Taking notes is the first step of the brainstorming process, it creates a frame of reference that can be utilized to envision a rough idea and scope of the project.

...In a rich man's world

After the baselines have been worked out, a wage or overall cost will be negotiated. The price can vary depending on the type and length of the project.

A prototype of sorts

As soon as the first brainstorming has been concluded, I will create a visual of a premature version of the final product. You will be welcomed to point out any flaws or errors that will be changed in the next revision.

Could you maybe change that...

Everyone has their own opinion, which is why designs often do not meet expectations. That is where revisions come in. Although it is mostly dependent on the project, I usually try to keep the number of revisions as low as possible to save your and my time.

All good things must come to an end

In the final steps of our procedure, you will acqurire your order and hopefully have a smile on your face when it arrives.

  • Discuss Establishing your preferences.
  • Creative concept A sketch of the final product.
  • Revisions Incorporating your new found ideas and thoughts.
  • Happy clients Finalization and delivery of the product.
