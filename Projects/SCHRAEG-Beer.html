Introduction

Through the course of a long-term university project we were tasked to create a corporate design for a fictional craftbeer company. A corporate design is the graphic identity of a company, it consists of templates and a basic layout that all follow the same theme and create a unified and professional appearence. We were mostly given creative freedom concerning the name, logo and label design of the company with the only exception being defined guidelines for final submission. The final submission itself was made up of a logo, a keyvisual, the company's business card, a blank letter template and the actual beer label.

Working Process

After a short brainstorming session I started drawing up a few sketches for my design and eventually settled on three main contenders which you can see below. Because I was aiming at a more none-traditional design targeting a young audience, I chose the second design to finalized. I should mention that the second option is rather a sketch of the beer label (left) and key visual (right) and not the actual logo, the logo itself was later designed around this concept design. The next step was to create a more defined version of my sketch. For this process I mainly used a combination of Adobe software such as Adobe Illustrator and Photoshop.
