Commissioned Art

Abitur Logo

Introduction

In this project I was tasked with designing a logo for our graduation with the theme being "Westminster Abi Zeit für die Krönung", which is german and translates to "Westminster Abi Time for the coronation".

Work Process

After a quick google search on the subject, I stumbled on the Logo of the City of Westminster . Seeing it as a fitting inspiration for my design I decided to base my project around this logo. The next step was to seperate the design into its major components being the emblem, the two lions and the banner.
